Overview

What is Kling 3.5

Kling 3.5 is an AI video generator for creators, marketers, and teams that need directed short-form video output from prompts or reference images. It is closer to a motion drafting workflow than a basic animation toy: describe the scene, guide the camera, refine the take, and keep the version that fits your project.

Search intent around Kling 3.5 often overlaps with Kling 3, Kling v3.5, kling ai 3.5, Kling 3.5 free, Kling 3.5 Pro, and Kling 3.5 video. This page is built to cover those entry points with clearer product context.

Workflow

How Kling 3.5 works

The workflow is easiest to understand as a short directorial loop: define the shot, set the motion controls, review the render, and keep refining until the clip is usable.

1

Set the scene

Write the subject, action, mood, camera angle, and framing in plain language.

2

Add a visual anchor

Upload a still image when you need the product, character, or composition to stay closer to the source.

3

Choose motion settings

Select aspect ratio, quality, duration, and the camera movement or pacing you want to test.

4

Render and refine

Review the take, adjust the prompt, and keep the strongest version for your campaign or content plan.
