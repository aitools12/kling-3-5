## Why use Kling 3.5?

Kling 3.5 is useful when someone needs motion output quickly instead of planning a full edit from the start. A creator can move from a scene idea to a first clip, compare multiple directions, and keep refining prompts or references until the strongest version is clear.

Common reasons people look for Kling 3.5 include:

- generating AI video drafts for product launches, paid ads, and landing pages

- creating client pitch clips that communicate direction faster than static boards
- exploring multiple creative directions before spending more on editing or post
